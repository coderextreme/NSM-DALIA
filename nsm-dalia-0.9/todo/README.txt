* "What Christians Believe for Open Research 20190204.txt" is a minimal english example of what christians believe.
* "What Christians Believe No Nulls.txt" is a minimal english example of what christians believe with nulls stripped.
* run.pl is an attempt to translate "What Christians Believe No Nulls.txt" to Tok Pisin
* stripnulls.pl strips nulls from stdin and writes the results on stdout, useful for taking nulls out of "What Christians Believe for Open Research 20190204.txt"
* TowersOfHanoi is the start of an example of converting Towers of Hanoi to English NSM exponents
