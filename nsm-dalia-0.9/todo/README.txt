* bible.txt is a minimal english example of what christians believe.
* biblenonnull.txt is a minimal english example of what christians believe with nulls stripped.
* run.pl is an attempt to translate biblenonnull.txt to Tok Pisin
* stripnulls.pl strips nulls from stdin and writes the results on stdout, useful for taking nulls out of bible.txt
* towers.txt is the start of an example of converting Towers of Hanoi to English NSM exponents
