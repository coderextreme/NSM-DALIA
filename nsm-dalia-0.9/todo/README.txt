* MinimalEnglishNewTestament is a minimal english example of what christians believe.
* MENewTestamentNoNulls is a minimal english example of what christians believe with nulls stripped.
* run.pl is an attempt to translate MENewTestamentNoNulls to Tok Pisin
* stripnulls.pl strips nulls from stdin and writes the results on stdout, useful for taking nulls out of MinimalEnglishNewTestament 
* TowersOfHanoi is the start of an example of converting Towers of Hanoi to English NSM exponents
